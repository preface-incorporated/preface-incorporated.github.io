The Autobookshelf

The Autobookshelf is the first product manufactured by Preface Inc.

It is a specialized bookshelf which:

  • Utilizes robotic arms for the purpose of grabbing books.
  • Contains a built-in digital catalog of the books ran on a miniature PC using Linux with info such as book name, author, ISBN number, publisher, etc.
  • And multiple smaller compartments for storing objects such as e-readers and other things, like magazines.
